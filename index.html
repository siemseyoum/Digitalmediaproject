Reflection.
The decision to focus on one particular question for the research paper was based on the inspiration of social media, specifically the impact of political leaders' active Twitter involvement. The project's overall aim is to explore how Twitter can be used in politics to engage the youth aged 18-24. The finding was quite astonishing.

What your key findings were and why they are important?
The summary of the research can be explained in two ways. The short answer, using humour to reach a younger audience has proven to be effective. The long answer, although creating accounts on any social media platform is a good start, the result would not be as effective to cause any engagement from the youth. The incentive has to come from within an existing group or community. The need to be part of something has always guided the younger audience. Trends or "going viral" is the manifestation of fun and entertaining information covered and powered by the desire to be a part of a community.

Throughout the findings, the most common answer to how the youth acts when they are faced with brand new information provided by the community. The majority answered by searching for more information to better understand the content. This is a vital piece of information, the key answer of the research was based on this data alone. This simple and innocent process is the gateway to getting engaged with politics

How you did it & what did you learn from it?
To begin explaining what went well, what did not and why. Reflect back on what skills became useful and what needed to be acquired to continue the research. Time management was the biggest factor when meeting deadlines making it the top priority. Good communication skills became essential when collecting qualitative data. Participants sometimes needed clarification on what the question trying to ask. From this experience, the acquired skills were to simplify and specify the intention behind each question.

The biggest that faced the project was expecting everything to fall into place without any mishaps. The data was taking longer than anticipated which resulted in rescheduling the deadline for each task. Two days were lost awaiting the result. This experience was a lesson to have alternatives/ backup plans to replace the upcoming task.
